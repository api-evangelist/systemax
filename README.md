# Systemax

Systemax Inc. (now operating as Global Industrial Company, NYSE: SYX) is a B2B direct marketer of industrial, maintenance, repair, and operations (MRO) products in North America. The company sells storage and shelving, material handling equipment, safety products, tools, HVAC, office supplies, and thousands of other industrial products to businesses, educational organizations, and government entities through its Global Industrial brand. Systemax was founded in 1949 and rebranded its primary segment to Global Industrial in 2021.

**Website:** [https://www.globalindustrial.com/](https://www.globalindustrial.com/)
**Investor Relations:** [https://investors.globalindustrial.com/](https://investors.globalindustrial.com/)

## Overview

Systemax/Global Industrial operates as a direct marketer of B2B industrial supplies with multi-channel distribution including e-commerce websites, direct sales, and catalogs. Their primary brands include:

- **Global Industrial** - industrial and MRO supplies
- **MISCO** - technology products (European markets)
- **Inmac Wstore** - technology products (European markets)

The company does not currently offer a public developer API or documented REST API for external integrations.
